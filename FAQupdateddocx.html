Sales Rep Dashboard


My /All views

  1. Q: What is the difference between My and All?

A: My displays all Opportunities for the Sales Campaign(s) to which you have been allocated that have your sales code. All displays all Opportunities for all members of the team for the Sales Campaign(s) to which you been allocated.

  1. Q: I cannot see my campaign in the Sales Rep Dashboard?

A: Ask your manager to add you as a resource to that that campaign 

  1. Q: What does False/False mean in the column Do Not Call/email

A: False/False = Do not Call/Do not Email

     True/True = Yes you can call / Yes you can email        


Current Registrations

  1. Q: What are Current Registrations?

A:  a Report that displays all contacts registered for campaign selected in the Sales Rep Dashboard


  1. Q: Can I export Current Registrations?

A: No. If you need a list of Sponsors etc., this can be provided by your manager

Columns

  1. Q: How do I manage the order of my columns?

 A:  Actions >> Columns you can manage the order of the column here


  1. Q: I cannot see the same columns as my colleague?

 A:  Actions >> Columns you can manage column displayed here


  1. Q: I have moved my columns in the sales rep dashboard. How can I save the changes?

A:  Actions >> Report >> Save As


  1. Q: I previously saved my columns but have made some changes and want to save the report under the same name?

A:  Actions >> Report >> Save


  1. Q: Can I save more than one column layout?

A:  Yes, just save the column layout with a new name


  1. Q: How do I delete a saved column layout?

A:  Yes. Select the report you want to delete and then select Actions>>Report>> Delete


  1. Q: Can I save the layout with the filters?

A:  Yes. Save as described above

Routing

  1. Q: How do I route more than one opportunity at a time?

A:  Select the checkbox(es) for the opportunities you want to route and click Route

  1. Q: I have routed some opportunities and cannot see them anymore?

A:  Once an Opportunity is routed, it moves to another campaign and you will no longer have visibility of those records

Mark as Lost

  1. Q: How do I Mark as Lost more than one opportunity at a time?

A:  Select the checkbox(es) for the opportunities you want to route and click Route

Creating Opportunities

  1. Q: How do I cancel a search for a Contact?

A: You cannot cancel a search whilst the search wheel is spinning. If you close the window the search will continue in the background. Please wait for the wheel to stop spinning before closing or running another search



  1. Q: How do I create more than one Opportunity at a time?

A: After searching for the contacts, you can select up to 7 contact records to create opportunities for the selected campaign


  1. Q: I have searched for an Account Name, but the search is taking too long. How can I speed this up?

A: Adding the Country of the Account to the search will speed up the results
